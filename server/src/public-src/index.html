About Us

About App Real Estate

Established in 2016, App Real Estate is a dedicated business brokerage specialising in the sale and acquisition of businesses in retail, hospitality, wholesale, and service sectors. With over a decade of operation, we have built a strong understanding of market dynamics, valuation strategies, and buyer behaviour, allowing us to position businesses effectively and manage transactions with clarity and confidence.

FAQ

Why should I choose your brokerage to sell my business?

We combine accurate valuations, targeted buyer networks, and confidential marketing with hands-on support from listing through to settlement. We work with a diverse network of buyers and sellers across various cultural and business backgrounds, ensuring an inclusive, respectful, and well-aligned transaction that achieves the best possible outcome.

How do you value my business?

We assess financial performance, industry benchmarks, growth potential, and market demand to provide a realistic, data-driven valuation.

How do you keep the sale confidential?

We use controlled marketing, anonymous listings, and only share sensitive information with qualified buyers under NDA.

How long does it take to sell a business?

The process typically takes between 8 to 16 weeks, with a structured approach to maintain momentum and achieve a strong outcome.

What support do you provide during the sale?

We manage the entire sales process end-to-end, from preparing your business and guiding valuation to marketing, buyer outreach, and screening. We handle enquiries, coordinate inspections, support negotiations, and assist with key steps such as lease transfer applications—ensuring a smooth path through to settlement.
