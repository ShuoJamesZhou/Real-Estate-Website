Privacy Policy

At APP REAL ESTATE, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of information provided by customers using our website and services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Website Usage Information

When you visit our website, certain anonymous information may be collected automatically for statistical and analytical purposes, including:

  • Number of visitors to the website
  • Date and time of visits
  • Pages viewed
  • General traffic patterns and website usage

This information does not identify individual users. It is used solely to help us improve website performance, functionality, and the services we provide.

Personal Information Collection

The type of personal information we collect depends on the products or services you engage with through APP REAL ESTATE.

When you contact us, submit an enquiry, or use our services, we may collect information such as:

  • Full name
  • Residential or business address
  • Email address
  • Contact phone number

This information is collected so we can communicate with you regarding your enquiry, requested services, or related matters.

We will only use your personal information for purposes directly related to the services we provide or where you would reasonably expect us to use it.

From time to time, APP REAL ESTATE may also use your information to provide updates about relevant products, services, or opportunities that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of these communications at any time.

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may only be disclosed where required by law or where there is a legal obligation to do so.

Email Communications

If you provide us with your email address, we may send you:

  • Service-related updates
  • Company announcements
  • Property or business opportunities
  • Promotional communications and special offers

We only send marketing communications to individuals who have provided consent to receive them. Every commercial email sent by APP REAL ESTATE will include an option to unsubscribe from future communications.

Security of Information

APP REAL ESTATE takes reasonable steps to protect the personal information we collect.

Information submitted through our website is transmitted through secure systems using encryption technology to help protect data during transmission over the Internet.

Any information stored by APP REAL ESTATE is protected through appropriate security measures, including:

  • Secure password protection
  • User authentication systems
  • Restricted access controls
  • Other reasonable security procedures

Where information is collected by authorised third-party providers on behalf of APP REAL ESTATE, secure data handling practices are also maintained.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact APP REAL ESTATE directly.
